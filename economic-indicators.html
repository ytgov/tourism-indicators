Economic Indicators

This section provides an overview of the economic indicators for the Yukon, such as GDP, Revenue, Jobs, labour income, and visitor spending.

Highlights
  • Gross business revenue reached a record high of $472.8 million in 2023, an 8.4% increase from the pre-pandemic peak of $436.2 million in 2019.
  • Projections based on spending data from The Data Collective's Lodging Aligned Spend Report (LASR) suggest that gross business revenue attributed to tourism is poised to reach an all-time high in 2024.

Notes: Target Revenue is from the Yukon Tourism Development Strategy, 2016. Actual Revenue comes from Yukon Business Survey, 2014-2022. Estimated and forecast revenue is calculated based on monthly spending from LASR.

Source: Statistics Canada, Tables: 24-10-0053-01, 24-10-0050-01. Updated Weekly.
