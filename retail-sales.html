Highlights
  • 2024 is on track to be a record year for sales at retail stores in the Yukon.
  • July and August had 3.6% and 8.4% less retail sales compared to the same months in 2023.
  • Growth has slowed since 2023, with a 2.2% y/y increase YTD for January to October 2024.
  • From 2020 to 2023, retail sales grew consistently, averaging about 6.7% annual growth.
  • The largest annual increase in this period was 7.8% in 2021, reflecting a post-pandemic recovery.

This dashboard presents insights into monthly retail sales in the Yukon, based on data from the Monthly Retail Trade Survey. The survey collects data on sales, e-commerce sales, and the number of retail locations by province, territory, and selected census metropolitan areas from a sample of retailers.

Data Source: Statistics Canada. Table 20-10-0056-01 Monthly retail trade sales by province and territory (x 1,000)
