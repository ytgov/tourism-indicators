Highlights
  • 2024 is on track to be a record year for retail sales in Yukon.
  • Retail sales in July and August were down by 3.6% and 8.4% compared to the same months in 2023.
  • Growth has slowed since 2023, with a 2.2% year-over-year increase from January to October 2024.
  • From 2020 to 2023, retail sales grew steadily, averaging about 6.7% growth per year.
  • The biggest yearly increase was 7.8% in 2021, showing a strong recovery after the pandemic.

Notes on data: Numbers are not adjusted for inflation. Numbers are not seasonally adjusted. This dashboard presents insights into monthly retail sales in the Yukon, based on data from the Monthly Retail Trade Survey. The survey collects data on sales, e-commerce sales, and the number of retail locations by province, territory, and selected census metropolitan areas from a sample of retailers.
