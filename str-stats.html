Highlights
  • Occupancy rates have decreased as the number of available listings has increased considerably over time.
  • Average Daily Rate (ADR) during summer months has seen a significant increase of 41%, rising from $170 in 2019 to $240 in 2024.
  • Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR) has grown by 32%, climbing from $125 in 2019 to approximately $165 in 2024.
  • These trends indicate a growing supply of short-term rentals, accompanied by strong demand and higher pricing power in the market.
