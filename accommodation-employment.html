Highlights
  • Jobs in the accommodation and food services industry have stayed fairly steady over time.
  • Employment in this sector has grown since the pandemic but is still below the 2016 peak of 2,013 jobs.
  • Weekly earnings increased by 8.2% from January to October 2024 compared to the same period in 2023.
