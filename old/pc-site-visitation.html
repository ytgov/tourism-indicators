Visits to National Parks and Historic Sites

Yearly data shows the number of visitors to key national parks and historic sites in the Yukon.

Highlights
  • Klondike Historic Site set an all time record in 2022 with 60,056 visits.
  • Kluane National Park visits have been trending downwards since a peak in the 1980s and 1990s.
  • S.S. Klondike is undergoing extensive restoration and lead removal work. As this is a long term project, the boat is not accessibnle to visitors at this time.
  • Chilkoot Trail has been partially closed since the 2020 pandemic, and a major flood washed out large sections of the trail in fall 2022.
