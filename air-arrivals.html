Highlights
  • 2024 was a record year for arrivals at Whitehorse International Airport, with a 22% increase over last year.
  • In August 2024, Yukon hit an all-time high with 26,982 passengers arriving at Erik Nielsen Whitehorse International Airport. This is 11.6% more than the pre-pandemic peak of 24,165 passengers in July 2019.
  • Despite upgrades to the airport in 2024, tourist arrivals were not affected because air traffic was redirected to a second runway.
