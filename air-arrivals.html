Highlights
  • 2024 is on track to be a record year for arrivals at Whitehorse International Airport, with a 20.3% increase year over year from January to September.
  • In August 2024, Yukon set an all time record of 26,982 passengers deplaning at Erik Nielsen Whitehorse International Airport.
  • This is an increase of 11.6% from the pre-pandemic high of 24,165 in July 2019.
  • In 2024, upgrades to the Whitehorse International Airport did not impede tourist arrivals, as air traffic was diverted to a second runway.
